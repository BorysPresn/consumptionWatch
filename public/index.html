About Consumption Watch

Consumption Watch is a modern solution for those who seek efficiency and economy in the management of their vehicle. This app is designed to give you complete control and transparency on your vehicle's fuel consumption and mileage.

Key features:

  • Fuel Consumption Tracking: Enter fueling data and let the app automatically calculate your average fuel consumption
  • Manage Mileage Records: Record your vehicle's mileage at each refueling for accurate statistics.
  • History & Stats: View your gas station history and mileage changes, and get summary statistics on fuel consumption and costs.

Our Mission:

We believe that managing fuel consumption doesn't have to be complicated. Consumption Watch is dedicated to making this process as simple and convenient as possible, allowing you to focus on the road, not the math.

Why choose us?

  • Ease of Use: An intuitive interface makes data management quick and easy.
  • Data accuracy: The app uses advanced algorithms to accurately calculate fuel consumption.
  • Security: Your data is secure and accessible only to you.

Feedback:

We are always open to feedback and are willing to improve Consumption Watch so that it meets your needs. Feel free to contact us with suggestions and comments.

Help & FAQ

Getting Started

Welcome to Consumption Watch! This application is designed to help you track your vehicle's fuel consumption and manage your driving data effectively. Here are a few steps to get you started:

  • Register an Account: Start by creating a new account or log in if you already have one.
  • Add a Record: Once logged in, you can start adding fuel consumption records by navigating to the "Add Record" section.
  • View History: Check your previous entries in the "History" section.
  • Analyze Statistics: Visit the "Statistic" section to see comprehensive stats about your fuel consumption.
